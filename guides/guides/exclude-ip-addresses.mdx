---
title: "Exclude IP Addresses"
---

You may wish to exclude your own website visits appearing on Averfo.

1. Navigate to your [website settings](https://averfo.com/websites).
1. Click the `Shields` tab.
1. If you are not sure what your IP address is, you can find it on an online search or via [this website](https://whatismyipaddress.com/). (It's the IPV4 address usually).
1. Add in your IP address into the exclusion field.
1. If you have more than one, make sure to separate each with a comma.
