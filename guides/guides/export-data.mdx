---
title: "Export Data"
---

To export your data to CSV, follow the steps below.

1. Navigate to your [website](https://averfo.com/websites) list and click `Reports`.
1. Scroll down to `Export Data`.
1. Set the date range that you would like to export.
1. Click `Request export` and check back later once processed.

<Note>
  Exports may take a few minutes depending on data size. Check back later or refresh the page to see if the download is ready.
</Note>
