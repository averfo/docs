---
title: "Reports"
---

You may wish to exclude your own website visits appearing on Averfo.

1. Navigate to your [website](https://averfo.com/websites) list and click `Reports`.
1. If you don't yet see any reports, make sure to go into the `Website settings` page and enable `Weekly Email Report` and/or `Monthly Email Report`.
1. Averfo will send you a concise email each week/month with a rundown of top locations, pages, pages to watch and areas of recommendation.
1. Previous `Reports` can be viewed at any time under the `Reports` page.
