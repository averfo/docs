Averfo is a privacy-first analytics platform to gather helpful metrics on your website without the need for a cookie banner. 

Averfo does not collect any cookies, ip-addresses or cross-site tracking data to ensure you are GDPR compliant.

With the use of AI, Averfo provides weekly and monthly bitesized reports to help understand which pages are performing well, and which need work.
